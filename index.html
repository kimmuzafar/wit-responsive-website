WIT At Glance

Al-Watania Poultry Institute of Technology (WIT) is a non-profitable institute. It has been established as a strategic partnership between the largest integrated poultry company in the Middle East, al-Watania Poultry (WP) and the government of Saudi Arabia, represented by TVTC (Technical & Vocational Training Corporation). This new entity, WIT, aims to provide world-class education and practical training of Saudi workforce in the field of poultry industries and its supporting services in order to assist them in securing employment in poultry industry sector.
